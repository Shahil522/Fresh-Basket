Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Check out our FAQ section for answers to common inquiries.
What payment methods are accepted at your store?

We accept a variety of secure and convenient payment methods, including:

  • 💳 Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, etc.)

  • 📱 UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, etc.)

  • 🏦 Net Banking (all major Indian banks)

  • 💼 Wallets (Paytm, Mobikwik, etc.)

  • 💵 Cash on Delivery (COD) – Available in select locations

  • 💳 EMI/Pay Later options (if enabled through payment gateways)

Can I return or exchange an item I've purchased from your online store?

Yes, we offer returns or exchanges on eligible items. Please note the following:

  • 🥦 Perishable items (like fresh fruits & vegetables) can only be returned if they are damaged, spoiled, or incorrect at the time of delivery.

  • 📦 Packaged or non-perishable items may be eligible for return or exchange within 24–48 hours of delivery, provided they are unopened and in original condition.

  • 🧾 Please keep your order receipt or confirmation number handy when requesting a return.

How long does it take to process and ship my order?

We aim to deliver your order as quickly and freshly as possible! Here’s how our processing and delivery works:

  • 🕒 Order Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 1–2 hours of confirmation.

  • 🚚 Delivery Time:

    • Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before [your cutoff time, e.g., 5:00 PM]

    • Orders placed after the cutoff will be delivered the next day

  • 📍 Delivery times may vary slightly depending on your location and product availability.

You’ll receive an update via SMS or email once your order is out for delivery.
